Research-Proven Benefits of Sound Bowl Meditation

Studies have demonstrated the profound impact of sound bowl meditation on mental and physical well-being:

  • Goldsby et al. (2017) found that sound bowl meditation significantly reduced tension, anxiety, and depression while enhancing spiritual well-being and mental clarity.
  • Saskovets et al. (2024) identified physiological benefits such as improved heart rate, lowered blood pressure, and enhanced breathing patterns.

These findings convey that sound bowl meditation is a powerful, low-cost, and accessible wellness tool that promotes holistic healing by aligning the body's energy centers (chakras), fostering inner peace and fulfillment.

Surveys/Feedback

Our survey results highlight strong potential for introducing new users to sound bowl meditation:

In round 1 of our survey, we received 21 responses:

90.5% of respondents had not participated in a Sound Bowl session before, presenting an opportunity to introduce new users.

Over 71.4% of respondents expressed interest in Sound Bowl experiences (through lessons, streaming, etc)

Various purposes respondents shared of using sound bowl for therapy/relaxation included stress relief, relaxation, and sleep

The top-ranked difficulties experienced with other music therapy apps/services included lack of motivation, difficult navigation, and cost of services

In round 2 of user feedback surveys, we shared our prototype with some users. Here is some feedback from testimonies:

Introducing: The Bright Side

Engagement

To maintain user engagement, The Bright Side will incorporate an AI DJ that users can chat with, making it more interactive. Along with setting reminders to encourage regular use and foster a relaxing routine.

Personalization of App

The app offers personalized suggestions for music and sessions based on the user's emotional state or goals, enhancing the relevance. Furthermore, users will be able to set their own goals to track their progress.

Interest-Centered Design

The app will be designed with a focus on the interests of its users, ensuring that the sound bowl meditation sessions are diverse and adaptable to various needs. Users select what resonates with them, ensuring that it meets their standards.

Low-Cost and Accessibility

The Bright Side will be available for free, making sound bowl meditation accessible to a wide audience. Users can enjoy high-quality sessions without financial barriers.

Scientific Backing

The app will emphasize its scientific foundation, with evidence-based research supporting the benefits of sound bowl meditation for mental health and wellness.
